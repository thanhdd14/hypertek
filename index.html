About us

HYPERTEK is a technology-driven company founded by a group of experienced professionals with a successful background in information technology and outsourcing. Our main target markets are Japan, Hong Kong, and the United States, where we have developed a distinctive approach to project completion.

Functioning as a technology center, HYPERTEK utilizes a network of specialized independent firms to generate impactful collaborations. This cooperative strategy enables us to access top talent across various technological fields such as Blockchain, AI, Machine Learning, AR, gaming, and embedded systems. In these areas, we emphasize establishing strong alliances with industry experts and leaders.

Moreover, to ensure smooth project implementation, HYPERTEK maintains a core team of skilled professionals dedicated to project management, supervision, and issue resolution.
